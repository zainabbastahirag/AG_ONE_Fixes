📖 Baba G Explains: What is ChatGPT?

No jargon. 5 bullets. Done.

  1. ChatGPT is a chatbot. You type a question, it types back an answer. Like texting a very knowledgeable friend.
  2. It's made by a company called OpenAI. It's not a person. It's software trained on lots of text from the internet.
  3. It can help you write, explain, brainstorm. Emails, essays, code, recipes — it tries to help with almost anything.
  4. It can be wrong. Baba G always says: double-check important things. Don't trust it for medical, legal, or financial decisions.
  5. Free and paid versions exist. Free is good for most people. Paid (GPT-4) is smarter and faster.
🙏 Baba G says: "ChatGPT is a helpful tool, beta. Use it like a smart assistant — not like an expert doctor or lawyer."

Is This Bank SMS a Scam?

Fake bank messages are the #1 scam. Here's what Baba G looks for.

🚨 Red flags in bank SMS scams

  • Asks you to click a link to "verify" your account
  • Asks for OTP, PIN, or password
  • Says your account will be blocked TODAY
  • Uses generic greeting: "Dear Customer" not your name
  • Link goes to a strange URL, not your bank's real website

✅ What real banks do

  • Never ask for OTP via SMS reply
  • Never ask you to click links to "unblock" account
  • Use your name or partial account number
🙏 Baba G says: "Beta, if any message asks for OTP — delete it immediately. Call your bank using the number on the back of your card, not from the SMS."

✉️ Resignation Letter Writer

Quitting your job? Baba G writes it professionally and politely.

Tips from Baba G

  • Keep it short — 3 to 5 sentences is enough
  • Give your last working date clearly
  • Don't badmouth the company — you may need a reference
  • Offer to help with handover
  • Send to your direct manager first, then HR

Example resignation letter

Subject: Resignation — [Your Name]

Dear [Manager Name],

I am writing to formally resign from my position at [Company Name], with my last working day being [Date].

I am grateful for the opportunities during my time here and will ensure a smooth handover.

Thank you for your support.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
